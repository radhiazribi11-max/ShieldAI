Privacy & Data Protection

1. Zero-Retention Policy

ShieldAI does NOT store your prompts, AI responses, or any personal data processed through our Gateway. We act as a "pass-through" layer only.

2. Local PII Scrubbing

Our redaction logic (PII Scrubbing) occurs before data reaches any Large Language Model (LLM). Sensitive identifiers like Emails and API Keys are masked at the edge.

3. Data Encryption

All communications between your browser and ShieldAI are encrypted using industry-standard TLS 1.3/AES-256 protocols.
